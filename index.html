Frequently Asked Questions

We offer tape in hair extensions, keratin bond extensions, micro link extensions, hand tied extensions, and weft extensions. Each method is professionally applied by our expert hair extension stylists.
Our long lasting hair extensions typically last 2-4 months depending on the method and care. Tape in extensions need repositioning every 6-8 weeks, while keratin bonds last 3-4 months.
When applied and maintained properly by professional hair extension stylists, extensions should not damage your hair. We use gentle application methods and premium quality extensions for the healthiest results.
